# Create a Marketing List in Dynamics 365 (Sales)

---

## 1. High-Level Overview

### What this is  
A **marketing list** is a saved group of contacts, leads, or accounts in Dynamics 365.  
It lets you organise people into one place so you can target them easily.

### Why it matters (for sales)  
- You can group prospects by campaign, industry, or interest  
- You avoid repeatedly searching for the same people  
- You can hand off clean, targeted lists to marketing or use them yourself  

### What problem it solves  
Instead of manually picking people every time, you create a list once and reuse it.  
This saves time and keeps your targeting consistent.

---

## 2. Word Glossary

| Term | Simple Meaning |
|-----|------|
| Marketing List | A saved group of people (contacts, leads, or accounts) |
| Member | A person or company inside the list |
| Static List | A fixed list that you manually control |
| Dynamic List | A list that updates automatically based on rules |
| Contact | A known person (usually already engaged) |
| Lead | A potential customer not yet qualified |
| Account | A company or organisation |

**Important:**  
Use these terms consistently. Don’t mix “contacts” and “leads” unless you mean to.

---

## 3. Quick Wins / Need to Know

- Use a **Static List** if you want full control  
- Use a **Dynamic List** if your audience changes often  
- You can only base a list on **one type** (Contacts *or* Leads *or* Accounts)  
- Lists can be reused across campaigns and activities  
- Keep list names clear (e.g. *“UK Retail Prospects – Q2”*)

---

## 4. Step-by-Step Guide

### Goal: Create a simple marketing list and add members

---

### Step 1: Go to Marketing Lists
- Open Dynamics 365  
- Navigate to **Marketing Lists** (usually under Sales or Marketing area)

---

### Step 2: Create a New List
- Click **New**
- Fill in:
  - **Name** → Make it clear and specific  
  - **Targeted At** → Choose one:
    - Contacts  
    - Leads  
    - Accounts  
  - **Type** → Choose:
    - Static (recommended for most sales use)

---

### Step 3: Save the List
- Click **Save**

This creates an empty list.

---

### Step 4: Add Members (People)

You now need to add people into the list.

Options:
1. **Add manually**
   - Click **Manage Members**
   - Choose **Add**
   - Select people from the system

2. **Use a filter (recommended for speed)**
   - Choose **Add using Advanced Find (or filter)**
   - Set simple conditions (e.g. Country = UK)
   - Apply results

---

### Step 5: Check Your List
- Open the list  
- Confirm members are correct  
- Remove anyone irrelevant

---

### Step 6: Use the List
You can now:
- Use it in campaigns  
- Assign activities (calls, emails)  
- Share with marketing  

---

## 5. Gotchas / Common Mistakes

- **Wrong audience type**
  - You cannot mix Leads and Contacts in one list  
  - Fix: create separate lists

- **Using Dynamic when you don’t need it**
  - Dynamic lists can change unexpectedly  
  - Use Static unless you *need* automation

- **Poor naming**
  - “List1” is useless later  
  - Be specific and consistent

- **Overcomplicated filters**
  - Keep filters simple  
  - Complex logic often pulls the wrong people

- **Forgetting to refresh**
  - Static lists don’t update automatically  
  - You must add/remove members manually

---

## 6. Summary

- A marketing list is a reusable group of people  
- It helps you target the right audience quickly  
- You create it once, then reuse it  
- Static lists give you control  
- Dynamic lists update automatically but need care  

**You should now be able to:**
- Create a list  
- Add the right people  
- Use it for sales activities

## 7. Simple Real-World Example

### Scenario: Targeting retail prospects in the UK

You want to call potential retail customers this quarter.

**Steps:**
1. Create list:  
   - Name: *“UK Retail Prospects – Q2”*  
   - Type: Static  
   - Audience: Leads  

2. Add members:
   - Filter:
     - Industry = Retail  
     - Country = United Kingdom  

3. Review:
   - Remove irrelevant leads  

4. Use:
   - Assign call tasks to yourself or team  
   - Track outreach consistently  

---

### Result

Instead of searching every time, you now have a clean, reusable list of the right prospects.

This saves time and keeps your outreach focused.
